John HirstThe Shortest History of European

约翰赫斯特分析历史的视角-选择三大元素演绎历史

The perspective of Dr. John Hirst analyzing history events is not from the order of time naturally, but capturing essential elements of European civilisation and to see how they have been reconfigured through from time. Elements make contributions to the spanking brief in this book, the Shortest History of European.

三大元素

There are three essential elements of European civilisation selected by the author—the culture of Anciet Greece and Roman, Christianity and German warriors. European civilisation is a mixture of the three elements.

  • 古希腊罗马智识
    One miracle in European civilisation is the intellectual endeavours of the Greeks in philosophy, literature, arts, maths, science, medicine, thinking about politics etc. The reason why the Greeks is so intellectual is still a clue.

  • 基督教
    Here is a miracle that Jews believes that there is only one god in the world contrasted to the Greeks and Romans’ common belief that there are many gods. And Jews had an extraordinary belief that this one god took special care to them and they were god’s chosen people. In return, they must keep god’s law, the Ten Commandants, which was given to them by Moses who led Jews out of the captivity of Egypt. In fact, Showing no worship to Roman emperor, Christians had been humiliated and been taken into exile by Roman government before the emperor, Constantine, became a Christian and gave official support to Christianity.

  • 日耳曼蛮族
    The illiterate German warriors were crazy about battles and destroyed the Roman Empire in AD 476 giving the fall of Roman.

    三大元素看待世界的方式

    The Greek view is that the world is simple, logical and mathematical. The Christian view is that the world id evil, and Christ alone saves. The German warriors’ view is that fighting is fun. Unlikely, european civilisation is the mixture of these three elements.
